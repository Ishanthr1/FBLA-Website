Arena Rental Information

Our versatile gymnasium is available for rent for various events, including:

  • Local Sporting Events
  • Live Concerts
  • Trade Shows
  • Community Gatherings
  • Private Events

With cutting-edge facilities, customizable seating arrangements, and top-tier amenities, our arena is the perfect venue for any occasion.

Additional services available:

  • Catering and concession stands

Rental Details
