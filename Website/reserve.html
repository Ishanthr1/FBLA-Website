Arena Rental Information

Our versatile gymnasium is available for rent for various events, including:

  • Local Sporting Events
  • Live Concerts
  • Trade Shows
  • Community Gatherings
  • Private Events

Rental Details

Our arena is equipped with:

  • High-quality sound and lighting systems
  • Seating for up to 3,500 people
  • State-of-the-art flooring suitable for various sports
  • Ample parking space

Rental Costs

Rental fees vary based on the event type and duration. Please contact us for a personalized quote. We offer flexible packages to suit your needs.

Booking Process

To reserve the arena for your event, please follow the steps below:

  1. Fill out the booking form below with your event details.
  2. We will confirm availability and provide a quote.
  3. Once confirmed, sign the rental agreement and make a deposit.
  4. Your event is officially booked!
